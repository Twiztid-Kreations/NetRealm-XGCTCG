1. Silver Screen Reverie
Casting Cost: 2MM (2 Generic, 2 Movie Energy)
Rarity: Rare
Ability: At the beginning of your upkeep, draw a card if you control at least three Relics.
Flavor Text: "In the glow of the silver screen, imagination becomes reality."
Prompt: A grand movie theater from the 1920s stands illuminated in a dark cityscape, its towering marquee glowing with golden letters that pulse faintly. Inside, the seats are empty, but a surreal projection spills from the screen—scenes of fantastical realms and epic battles overlapping and blending together in the air. Wisps of ethereal light flow from the screen, coalescing into shimmering shapes of creatures and relics. A single reel of film spirals upward, glowing faintly as if alive.
